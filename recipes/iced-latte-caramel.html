Iced Latte Caramel

A coffee drink that contains milk and salted caramel syrup.

Ingredients:

  • Double shot espresso (50-60mL)
  • Aerated milk (100mL)
  • Salted caramel syrup (20mL)
  • Ice (100g)

Steps:

  • Place all the ice in a glass.
  • Extract the espresso and pour it over the ice.
  • Add the aerated milk.
  • Finish with the salted caramel syrup.
